Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know when my order is ready?
Check the "Your Past Orders" section on the right. The status will change from 'New' to 'Ready'. We also try to announce order IDs at the collection window.
Can I modify an order after checking out?
Once checked out, orders cannot be modified. Please ensure your order is correct before pressing "Checkout." If there is an immediate error, please speak to staff directly.
Where do I pay and collect my food?
Payment and collection are handled at the main canteen window. Please provide your Order ID (e.g., #A1B2C3D4) for faster service.
